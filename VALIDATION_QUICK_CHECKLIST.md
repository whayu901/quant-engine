## CRITICAL SUCCESS FACTORS

**Honesty in Interviews:**
- [ ] Ask open questions ("What's your biggest pain?")
- [ ] Don't suggest answers ("Is code-mixing a problem?")
- [ ] Don't pitch product ("We handle code-mixing!")
- [ ] Listen 70%, talk 30%
- [ ] Capture unprompted feedback only

**Data Quality:**
- [ ] All interviews recorded + transcribed
- [ ] Scorecards filled out immediately (memory fresh)
- [ ] Quotes saved verbatim
- [ ] No interpretation/bias in analysis
- [ ] Double-check math

- [ ] Complete Week 1 by July 7 (no extensions)
- [ ] Complete Week 2 by July 14 (no extensions)
