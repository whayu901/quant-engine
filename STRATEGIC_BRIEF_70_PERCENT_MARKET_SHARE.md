- **Mobile:** <50KB payloads (vs 200KB+), offline-capable - 70% of SEA researchers mobile-first

### 2. Price Disruption (ATTACK VECTOR)
- **50% cheaper:** $49-149/user vs $99-500 (CoLoop/Dovetail)
- **Regional pricing:** 20-30% discount in Indonesia/Thailand/Philippines
- **Enterprise flexibility:** Custom pricing wins large deals

### 3. Ecosystem Lock-In (RETENTION MOAT)
- **Integrations:** SurveyToGo, tSurvey, WhatsApp (SEA-specific tools)
- **Network effects:** Cross-project knowledge base, shared templates
- **Switching costs:** Data migration complexity + team adoption

**Time to Replicate:** 12-18 months for CoLoop/Dovetail to match
