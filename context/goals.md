# Goals

## North star
Build the AI platform for **market-research operations in Southeast Asia**, and use it to win
**Kadence International** — by owning the parts of the research workflow that CoLoop.ai and generic
AI tools don't touch.

## Direction A (locked 2026-06-30)
The CoLoop-parity qual platform is **already built** (Phase 0-7: auth, ingestion, analysis grid,
RAG/chat, quant, collaboration/enterprise, media, visualization). We treat that as a **table-stakes
base — keep it, but stop expanding it as the differentiator.** Energy now goes to the moat.

## What "winning" looks like (in order)
1. **Fieldwork QC / Verifier** built — proves data-collection integrity at scale; nothing CoLoop or
   any incumbent sells to Kadence. _(NOT built yet — the priority.)_
2. **SEA layer** real: code-mix transcription, in-region hosting + UU PDP/PDPA, local integrations
   (SurveyToGo / tSurvey / KOINS / WhatsApp).
3. _(Later)_ Beat CoLoop more broadly on qual + add quant depth — only after 1 and 2.

## Non-goals (do not drift into these)
- NO — expanding the existing qual-parity features as the differentiator. They're table-stakes, done.
- NO — "beating CoLoop on qual analysis" *right now*. That's a later phase.
- NO — a generic CoLoop clone (we already have the parity; the point is the moat on top).
- NO — gold-plating domain Skills / polish before the Fieldwork QC wedge exists.

Buyer = **Kadence's president director** (Wahyu's ex-boss). Pitch = "amplify your people, own a layer
no vendor covers," not "replace your researchers."
